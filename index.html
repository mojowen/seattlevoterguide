There are definitely concerns over what could happen if there is unlimited expansion. The City of Billings must have the ability to provide municipal services to those areas that are annexed without creating an unnecessary financial or resource burden for those areas already within the city limits. Pass a fully inclusive non discrimination ordinance? more info Yes

As the largest city in Montana we cannot afford to look to the past. Billings’ future is before us and it demands our attention. Our future is dependent upon enticing a diverse, talented work force, attractive to high tech businesses paying living wages, willing to invest in our shared future. By building a more diverse community, we attract high tech companies willing to engage the Montana work force stereotype, pay a living wage and ensure a motivated, innovative, and dedicated work force. To achieve this we must: - Continue to create an environmentally green city - Develop the city’s reputation as a diverse community - Ensure that all who live here have a voice - Implement ‘out of the box’ ideas to conserve water and reduce or delay the need for a new water processing plant - Plan the city’s future foot print to include affordable housing - Provide more information to the voters on all issues before them to allow for informed decisions One of Billing’s strengths is its diversity which will continue to grow, our goal should be to ensure each citizen has an equal voice at the table, feels safe in their neighborhoods and has equal access to all city services.

Yes

Expanding jail diversification programs would be a good thing. The American Medical Association determined alcohol addiction to be a disease in 1956. Many people with addictions (drug and alcohol) require treatment of one kind or another in order to remain drug and alcohol free. There are those professionals who believe that methamphetamine is a harder addiction to break and that in-patient treatment is a must for some individuals in order for them to be successful. Most certainly, Drug Treatment Courts have been successful, in good part because those involved with Drug Treatment Courts know that they are being monitored, and that there is available to them a strong support system to help them remain drug and/or alcohol free. Helping people remain drug free is a far cheaper way to deal with the issue of addicted people than incarceration. People who are drug/alcohol free can become contributing members of society.

Work to limit city expansion boundaries? more info No

The City Council cannot control subdivision growth outside the city limits. However, it is important that if subdivisions are annexed into the city, the subdivisions must pay appropriate impact fees that ensure the taxpayers of Billings aren’t footing the bill for infrastructure. Appropriate impact fees for annexation naturally make infill areas cost competitive for redevelopment, which, in turn, helps increase the tax base without expanding the service area.

This issue has no easy answer I will work with local groups to balance the need for housing and infrastructure
